Empowering Accessibility Through Technology

A comprehensive solution developed by VIT Pune AI & DS students to assist people with disabilities through voice, emotion, and sign language technologies.

Download Project View Demo

Key Features

Our solution integrates multiple accessibility technologies into a single platform

Voice Assistant

Intelligent voice commands for hands-free operation. Ask questions, set reminders, or control your device with natural speech.

Multi-Language Support

Supports multiple languages including English, Marathi, Hindi, and Kannada for broad accessibility.

Emotion Detection

Advanced AI analyzes text to detect emotions and adjusts speech output accordingly for more natural interactions.

Sign Language Conversion

Converts text to Indian Sign Language (ISL) videos to assist the hearing impaired community.

AI-Powered Assistance

Integrates with Cohere AI for intelligent question answering and task completion.

Real-time Information

Provides weather updates, time, date, jokes, and Wikipedia information through voice commands.
